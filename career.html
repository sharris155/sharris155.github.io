About Me

Ever since I was young, I've had a passion for designing and creating. I can trace this love back to when I used to play a game on Roblox called "Bloxburg." It was a game that allowed players to design and build their own houses, and I would spend hours every day designing beautiful, functional homes. That early experience sparked a deep fascination with architecture and design. It wasn’t just about placing walls and furniture—it was about creating spaces that felt personal and inviting. As I grew older, this interest evolved into a desire to pursue architectural engineering. I realized that architecture is not just about creativity but also about understanding the technical aspects that bring a design to life.

Career Paths in Architecture

Residential Architect

Specialize in designing homes, ensuring comfort and functionality, while reflecting the client's vision.

Commercial Architect

Focus on designing office buildings, shopping malls, and other commercial structures that meet business needs.

Urban Planning

Work on large-scale projects, such as city planning, parks, and infrastructure, to shape the urban environment.
